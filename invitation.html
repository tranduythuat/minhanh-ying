FAQs

Most international guets will need a visa to enter Vietnam.

Apply for e-visa here: https://evisa.gov.vn

Processing time: 3-7 business days
Validity: Up to 90 days ($25 single entry / $50 multiple entry)
What you'll need:

  • Passport valid for at least 6 months from entry date
  • A digital passport photo
  • A scanned copy of your passport information page
  • Entry/exit details (airport & dates)
  • Online payment (credit card)

Visa Exemption:

  • Citizens of certain countries may enter Vietnam visa-free for a limited stay. Check at https://evisa.gov.vn
  • Examples: ASEAN countries (varies by nationality), South Korea, Japan, several European countries
